Afghan Proverbs

Wisdom passed down through generations

A proverb is a short, meaningful sentence derived from the bitter and sweet experiences of people, shaped over time and passed down from generation to generation among the people of Afghanistan (and other nations).

آدم تنبل، عقل جهل وزير را دارد.

The lazy person has the mind of an ignorant minister.

به شتر مرغ كفتند بار ببر، كفت: مرغم، كفتند : بير، كفت : شترم!

They told the ostrich to carry a load, it said: “I’m a bird.” They told it to fly, it said: “I’m a camel!”

از ان نترس كه هاى وهوى دارد، از آن بترس كه سر به توى دارد!

Don’t fear the one who shouts and makes noise; fear the one who stays quiet and keeps their head down.

This website presents a collection of Afghan proverbs with the aim of preserving and promoting folk culture.
